ABOUT US:

Hey!! We are the Youngs – Joshua, Miranda, Maya, and the recently added Brisket.

We sold our house, our cars, and packed the rest up and moved from Chicago, IL *USA* to Copenhagen, Denmark in 2018.

Now that we are mostly settled in, we decided we’d start sharing our experiences as Americans living in Denmark. Check out our adventures, thanks for watching!
