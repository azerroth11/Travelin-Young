Watch us on Youtube to see how our new lives abroad help to keep us young.

Hey!! We are the Youngs:

Joshua, Miranda, Maya, and the recently added Brisket.

We sold our house, our cars, and packed the rest up and moved from Chicago, IL *USA* to Copenhagen, Denmark in 2018.

Now that we are mostly settled in, we decided we’d start sharing our experiences as Americans living in Denmark.

We currently post 2 videos each week, Tuesdays and Fridays.

Our Tuesday video is where we often talk about our lives in Denmark, experiences learning Danish, or basically about our lifestyle here.

The Friday video is where we try to get out and explore Denmark.
We do our best to visit new cities, museums, or other areas of interest around Denmark and abroad. Enjoy!

Check out our adventures, thanks for watching!
